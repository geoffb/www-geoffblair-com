About

I'm a game developer and co-founder of Lost Decade Games. I currently reside in Colorado Springs with my wife, Melissa Iris, and our cats.

After spending nearly a decade as a full stack web developer at companies such as Yahoo! Games (now defunct) and Raptr (also defunct), I decided to focus on my lifelong obsession with game development by founding Lost Decade Games with Matt Hackett in 2010. Matt and I hosted Lostcast, a long-running podcast about indie game development, with an HTML5 slant.

When not sitting behind a computer screen, I like to play disc golf, snowboard, travel, and play board games.
